About Our Chapter

Established

11th Dec 2024

Network

60+ Members

The ASME Student Chapter at Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research was officially established on December 11, 2024, marking a significant step toward empowering students in the field of mechanical engineering. This chapter aims to provide a dynamic platform for students to enhance their technical knowledge, develop leadership skills, and engage in industry-oriented activities such as workshops, seminars, and competitions.
